      Title: Mexican Salsa
 Categories: Appetizers, Mexican, Sauces, Relishes
      Yield: 8 servings

      8 oz Tomato sauce
      2 tb Crushed red chili
    1/2 ts Cumin powder
    1/2 ts Oregano
      1 ts Salt
      2    Garlic cloves, minced
      2 ts Vinegar
    1/2    Lemon, juiced

  Combine all ingredients and mix well. Let stand for 3 hours.
  Excellent with tacos or as a dip for tortilla chips.
