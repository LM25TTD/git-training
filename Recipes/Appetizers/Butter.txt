      Title: "Butter"
 Categories: Appetizers, Spreads, Dips, Vegan
      Yield: 1 servings

    3/4 c  Hot, cooked cornmeal
    1/2 c  Water
    1/4 c  Coconut
    1/2 ts Salt

  Blend all ingredients till smooth.  Use on bread & vegetables, but
  note that it does not melt well.

  JoAnn Racho, "Of These Ye May Freely Eat"
