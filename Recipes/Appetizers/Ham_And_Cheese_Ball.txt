      Title: HAM AND CHEESE BALL
 Categories: Appetizers
      Yield: 1 servings

      1 pk Smoked ham (3 oz.)
      1    Philadelphia Cream Cheese
           8 oz.
      1    Jar Old English sharp
           Cheddar cheese
      1 ts Onion powder
      1 pk Nuts

  Soften cheese to room temperature.  Sprinkle some of the onion powder over
  thinly sliced ham, and set aside.  Mix cheese and remainder of onion
  powder.  Chop ham finely, put nuts in blender.  Cover cheese ball with nuts
  and refrigerte.
