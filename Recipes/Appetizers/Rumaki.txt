      Title: Rumaki
 Categories: Appetizers, Meats
      Yield: 12 rumaki

      6    Chicken livers
           -- washed, dried, halved
      1 tb Horseradish mustard
      4 sl Bacon; cut into thirds
      6    Water chestnuts
           -- cut into halves

  Mix livers with horseradish mustard.  Wrap each bacon strip around a piece
  of liver and a water chestnut half.  Secure with a toothpick.

  Broil, turning frequently, until bacon is crisp, about 10 to 15 minutes.

  NOTE: Use round wooden toothpicks for this recipe.  Flat toothpicks may
  char during broiling.

  Calories per rumaki: About 30
