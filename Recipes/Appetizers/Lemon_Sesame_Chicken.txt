      Title: LEMON SESAME CHICKEN
 Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Appetizers
      Yield: 4 servings

      2    Whole skinnd chicken breasts
      1    Egg
      1 t  Water
    1/4 c  Flour
      1 t  Paprika
      1 t  Onion powder
    1/2 t  Poultry seasoning
      2 T  Oil
    3/4 c  Unsalted chicken broth
    1/4 t  Lemon peel
      1 T  Lemon juice
      1 T  Toasted sesame seeds

  Wash chicken and pat dry.  Beat egg and water.  Combine next four dry
  ingredients.  Dip chicken in egg, then flour. Brown in oil.  Add
  remaining ingredients except sesame seeds. Cover and simmer for 20
  minutes.  Remove chicken.  Thicken liquid with leftover flour mixture.
  Pour sauce over chicken and sprinkle with sesame seeds.  Makes 4
  servings.
