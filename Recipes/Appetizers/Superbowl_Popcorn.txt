      Title: SUPERBOWL POPCORN
 Categories: Appetizers
      Yield: 6 servings

      4 qt Popped corn
     12 oz Peanuts
      2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
    1/4 c  Melted butter
      2 t  Lemon pepper
      2 t  Cumin
    1/4 t  Cayenne pepper
      2 t  Chili powder
      1 t  Paprika

  Combine popcorn, peanuts, and cheese in a large bowl.  Set aside.  Add
  spices to melted butter one at a time, stirring constantly.  Pour over
  popcorn and toss to coat.  Enough for six.
