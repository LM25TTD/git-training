      Title: Cheese Chips
 Categories: Diabetic, Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
      Yield: 6 servings

    1/2 c  All purpose flour;                    pn Curry powder;
    1/2 c  Whole wheat flour;                    pn Salt;
    1/2 c  Parmesan cheese; grated             2 tb Butter;
    1/2 ts Baking powder;                      1 md Egg; lightly beaten
        pn Pepper;                             2 tb Water;

  Reserve 1 tablespoon of the all purpose flour for flouring board. In a
  bowl, combine remaining all purpose flour, whole wheat flour, cheese,
  baking powder, pepper, curry, and salt. With 2 knives, cut in butter until
  mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Combine egg and water; stir into flour
  mixture until mixture can be gathered into a ball of dough. Knead dough on
  a lightly floured board 5-6 strokes. Roll out to 1/8 inch thickness. Cut
  into 2 inch triangles. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 12-15 minutes or until
  lightly browned.

  Makes 36 chips, 6 servings 1 serving = 6 chips
  :           1 protein choice
  :           1 starchy choice
  :           15 g carbohydrate
  :            6 g protein
  :            6 g fat
  :          580 kilojoules
  :          138 calories
