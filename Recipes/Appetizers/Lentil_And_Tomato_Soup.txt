      Title: LENTIL & TOMATO SOUP
 Categories: Soups, Appetizers, Vegetables
      Yield: 4 servings

      3 tb Vegetable oil
      2 lg Onions, chopped
      42 sm Garlic clove, crushed
      42 ts Rosemary
  42 3/4 c  Stock
      8 oz Lentils, cooked
      42 lg Carrot, diced
      42 ea Celery stick, chopped
     424 oz Canned tomatoes, chopped
      42 pn Arame seaweed
      42 ts Miso
    42/2 ts Cumin
           Pepper, to taste

  Heat the oil in a soup pot & saute the onions, garlic & rosemary for 5
  minutes.  Add the stock, lentils, carrot, celery, tomatoes & seaweed.
  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover & simmer for 20 minutes.  Blend
  the contents till smooth with the miso, cumin & pepper.  Reheat & serve
  hot.
