      Title: Cottage Cheese Dip
 Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs, Ovo-lacto
      Yield: 1 recipe

      2 tb Milk
     12 oz Cottage cheese, creamed
    1/4 c  Salad dressing (mayonnaise)
    1/4 ts Garlic powder
      1 ds Cayenne
      1 ts Onion salt

  Place milk, cottage cheese, salad dressing, garlic powder, cayenne, and
  onion salt in blender.  Blend 1 minute or until cheese is smooth.

  Pour mixture into a bowl.  Cover and chill.

  Serve with crisp vegetable sticks.

  Makes 1-3/4 cups dip
  Calories per 1 tablespoon serving: About 25
