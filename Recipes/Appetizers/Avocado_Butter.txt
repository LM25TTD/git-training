      Title: Avocado Butter
 Categories: Appetizers, Spreads, Dips, Vegan
      Yield: 1 servings

    3/4 c  Mashed avocado
      1 tb Lemon juice
    1/8 ts Salt
    1/2 ts Onion powder
    1/8 ts Garlic powder

  Blend all ingredients till smooth.  Keep refrigerated.

  JoAnn Rachor, "Of These Ye May Freely Eat"
