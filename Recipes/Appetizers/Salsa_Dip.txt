      Title: Salsa Dip
 Categories: Dips, Appetizers, Untested
   Servings:  4

      4    Tomatoes (chopped/drained)
      1 cn Black olives (chopped)
      1 tb Olive oil
      1 tb Garlic salt
      2 pk Green onions (chopped)
      1 cn Green chilies (chopped)
      1 tb Vinegar

     Mix; refrigerate at least 1 hour (better overnight).  Serve with
  tortilla chips.
