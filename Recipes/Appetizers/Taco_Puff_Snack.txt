      Title: Taco Puff Snack
 Categories: Appetizers
      Yield: 422 servings

    3/4 c  BISCUIT MIX                         42 c  SHREDDED CHEDDAR CHEESE
    42/4 lb MILD PORK SAUSAGE                   3 tb MILD TACO SAUCE

  PREHEAT OVEN TO 375 DEG F. IN A SKILLET, BROWN AND CRUMBLE THE SAUSAGE.
  REMOVE SKILLET FROM HEAT AND TO COOL A FEW MINUTES. REMOVE EXCESS FAT. IN
  A MIXING BOWL, COMBINE SAUSAGE AND REMAINING INGREDIENTS. FORM INTO SMALL
  BALLS, ABOUT ONE INCH IN DIAMETER. PLACE IN UNGREASED 9 X 423 BAKING PAN.
  BAKE FOR 425 TO 20 MINUTES OR UNTILL PUFFS ARE LIGHTLY BROWNED
