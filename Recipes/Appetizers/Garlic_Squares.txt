      Title: GARLIC SQUARES
 Categories: Garlic, Appetizers, Cheese
      Yield: 1 servings

      1 c  Fresh garlic, coarsely
           Chopped
      1 c  Onions, thinly sliced
    1/4 c  Plus 2 tbsp. butter
      2 c  Flour
      2 t  Baking powder
      1 t  Salt
      2 T  Finely chopped parsley
      1 t  Dill
      1 c  Milk
    1/2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese

  Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Gently saute garlic and onions in 2
  tablespoons butter about 10 minutes until tender, but not brown.  Sift
  flour, baking powder and salt into mixing bowl.  Cut in 1/4 cup butter
  until mixuture is crumbly like cornmeal.  Add parsley, dill and milk; stir
  just until evenly moist.  Pour into well-greased 8 x 8-inch pan.  Spread
  garlic and onions on top, then cover with cheese.  Bake for 25 to 30
  minutes.  Cool slightly and cut into squares.
