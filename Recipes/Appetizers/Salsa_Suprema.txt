      Title: Salsa Suprema
 Categories: Appetizers, Mexican, Sauces, Relishes
      Yield: 16 servings

      1 lg Tomato, chopped
      1 md Onion, chopped
      2    Fresh green chilies, chopped
    1/2 ts Garlic salt
    1/2 ts Soy sauce
           Salt to taste

  Combine all ingredients and chill, covered, in refrigerator at least
  one hour.
