      Title: Cheese Dip
 Categories: Sharon, Appetizers
      Yield: 12 servings

      2 lb VELVEETA CHEESE                     1 cn ROTELL TOMATOES    **

  **   DRAIN AND CHOP TOMATOES         **
  MELT CHEESE IN MICROWAVE OR OVER LOW HEAT ON STOVE TOP. STIR IN TOMATOES
  AND HEAT MIXTURE THROUGH.
