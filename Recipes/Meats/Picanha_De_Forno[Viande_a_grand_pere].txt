Leandro

Ingredients:
1 Brazilian Picanha Fatty Cut
2 Kg Rock Salt
An oven tray

Preparation:
1) make a rock salt layer in the tray botton;
2) put the Picanha beef cut on this layer with the fat turned up;
3) cover the whole cut with rock salt. Make it form a shell;
4) Let cook in the oven for 45 minutes;
5) After is cooked, break the salt shell and put the beef out of the tray; 
6) Enjoy the softest beef in the world!
